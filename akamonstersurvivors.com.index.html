About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an addictive action survival game where your goal is simple: survive as long as possible against relentless waves of monsters. As the clock ticks, enemies become stronger and more numerous, testing your skills and strategic thinking.

Choose from different characters, each with unique abilities and attributes that drastically change your gameplay style. Collect experience gems to level up and select powerful upgrades that enhance your character's abilities or grant new ones altogether.

With its perfect blend of roguelike elements and action gameplay, Monster Survivors offers endless replayability as no two runs are ever the same. How long can you survive in this monster-filled world?

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from a diverse roster of characters, each with unique starting weapons and special abilities.

Power-Up System

Collect experience and level up to unlock powerful upgrades that transform your character during your run.

Endless Challenge

Face increasingly difficult waves of enemies as you progress, with difficulty scaling over time.

Leaderboards

Compete against players worldwide to achieve the highest scores and longest survival times.

Strategic Gameplay

Each run requires tactical decisions about upgrades, positioning, and enemy prioritization.

Boss Encounters

Periodically face powerful boss monsters that require special tactics and quick reflexes to defeat.

How to Play

Controls

  • WASD Move your character in all directions
  • Mouse Aim in specific directions (some weapons/abilities)
  • Space Use special ability (when available)
  • ESC Pause the game

Gameplay Tips

  • Prioritize movement speed upgrades to help you avoid enemy swarms
  • Collect all experience gems to level up faster and get more upgrades
  • Balance offensive and defensive upgrades for optimal survival
